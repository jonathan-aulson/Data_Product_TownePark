## Account Manager Responsibilities

### Site-Level Management
- **Site Performance Monitoring**: Daily monitoring of site performance metrics
- **Operational Oversight**: Oversight of daily site operations and performance
- **Staff Management**: Coordination with site staff and management
- **Customer Relationship Management**: Maintenance of customer relationships
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring service quality and customer satisfaction

### Financial Management
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Site-level revenue forecasting and analysis
- **Cost Management**: Monitoring and management of site costs
- **Budget Planning**: Site budget planning and variance analysis
- **Financial Reporting**: Regular financial reporting and analysis
- **Performance Analysis**: Analysis of financial performance and trends

### Data Management
- **Data Entry**: Accurate and timely data entry into systems
- **Data Validation**: Validation of data accuracy and completeness
- **Data Analysis**: Analysis of operational and financial data
- **Reporting**: Regular reporting of site performance and metrics
- **Documentation**: Maintenance of accurate records and documentation

## Forecasting Processes

- **Validation**: Validation of data accuracy and completeness
