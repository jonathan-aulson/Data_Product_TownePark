# Towne Park Product Roadmap

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the strategic product roadmap for Towne Park's financial systems modernization initiative, covering the development and implementation timeline for billing, forecasting, and contract management systems.

## Strategic Objectives

### Primary Goals
- **System Modernization**: Modernize legacy financial systems with cloud-based solutions
- **Process Automation**: Automate manual processes to improve efficiency and accuracy
- **Data Integration**: Integrate disparate data sources into unified systems
- **User Experience**: Improve user experience and system usability
- **Scalability**: Build scalable solutions to support business growth

### Success Metrics
- **Efficiency Gains**: 50% reduction in manual processing time
- **Accuracy Improvement**: 99.5% data accuracy in financial calculations
- **User Adoption**: 95% user adoption rate within 6 months
- **Cost Reduction**: 30% reduction in operational costs
- **System Reliability**: 99.5% system uptime

## Product Features and Capabilities

### Billing System (PowerBill)
- **Automated Invoice Generation**: Streamlined invoice creation and processing
- **Revenue Share Calculations**: Accurate revenue sharing calculations
- **Contract Integration**: Seamless integration with contract management
- **Customer Management**: Comprehensive customer account management
- **Financial Reporting**: Advanced financial reporting and analytics

### Forecasting System
- **Site Statistics Management**: Comprehensive site performance tracking
- **Payroll Forecasting**: Job family-based payroll planning
- **Revenue Forecasting**: Revenue projections and rate management
- **P&L Analysis**: Detailed profit and loss analysis
- **Performance Benchmarking**: Comparative performance analysis

### Contract Management System
- **Contract Lifecycle Management**: End-to-end contract management
- **Rate Management**: Dynamic rate management and escalations
- **Compliance Monitoring**: Automated compliance tracking
- **Performance Metrics**: Contract performance monitoring
- **Integration Capabilities**: Seamless system integration

## Technology Stack

### Platform Architecture
- **Microsoft Power Platform**: Core application development platform
- **Microsoft Dataverse**: Unified data platform
- **Power Apps**: User interface and application development
- **Power Automate**: Process automation and workflow management
- **Power BI**: Business intelligence and reporting
